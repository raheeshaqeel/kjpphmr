About Us

KJPP Herman Meirizki & Rekan

KJPP Herman Ruslim was established in September 2013 and obtained the Public Appraisal Services Office license from the Ministry of Finance on September 18, 2013, with License Number 3.13.0054.

Later, it transformed into KJPP Herman Meirizki and Rekan on January 11, 2014, and obtained the Public Appraisal Services Office Business License from the Minister of Finance on February 10, 2014, with License Number 2.14.0120.

Vision & Mission

Visi

To become the best and most professional Public Appraisal Services Office in providing services according to Indonesian Valuation Standards (SPI).

Misi

KJPP Herman Meirizki & Rekan is committed to providing independent, ethical, professional, and quality appraisal services while implementing risk management in appraisal services, resulting in quality and standard appraisal products.
